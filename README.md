</p>

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎵 **Audio Capture & Processing**  
  Load and preprocess washing machine sound recordings for analysis.

- 📊 **Sound Analysis & Visualization**  
  Visualize waveforms, spectrograms, and frequency distributions.

- 🤖 **Machine Learning Classification**  
  Classify washing cycles and detect anomalies using:
  - **SVM (Support Vector Machines)**
  - **Decision Trees**
  - **K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)**

- 📈 **Cycle Classification**  
  Identify different stages of washing (wash, rinse, spin) based on sound patterns.

- 🔬 **Data Export & Reporting**  
  Export analysis results for further study or comparison.
